How This Calculator Works

This tool estimates your Amazon FBA costs using 2026 fee rates:

  • Referral Fee: 8–45% depending on category (most categories: 15%)
  • FBA Fulfillment Fee: based on size tier and weight, starting at $3.22 for small standard items
  • Fuel Surcharge: 3.5% of the fulfillment fee (effective April 2026)
  • Storage Fee: $0.78/cubic ft (Jan-Sep) or $2.40/cubic ft (Oct-Dec) — estimated, assumes 1 month storage

Note: This is an estimate for planning purposes. Always confirm exact fees in your Amazon Seller Central account, as fees vary by exact product dimensions and category.
